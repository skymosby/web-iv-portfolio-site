About Me

I'm a communicator. I like to tell unique stories and stir up emotions. Not only do I enjoy the visual language of design, I'm also fascinated by the language of web! In fact, this website was coded from scratch with HTML, CSS, and a little Javascript.

I'm a drawer. I realized this in kindergarten when the teacher handed out markers and construction paper. My very first drawing was of a slide that I remembered seeing on the playground. I've spent most of my life pushing those drawing skills and it's been a tremendous gift when planning out design projects. Interpreting the world through illustration is something that fills me with unending joy.

I'm a designer. I find serenity in process work. Design has taught me that there is always another step further; there's always a better solution one or twenty thumbnails away. As a designer I'm always learning and improving.
